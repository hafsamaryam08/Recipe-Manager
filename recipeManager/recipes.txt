Recipe: Chocolate Chip Cookies
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
- 1\2 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1\2 cup white sugar
- 1\2 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1\2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1\2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1\2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degree Fahrenheit.
2. Cream together butter, white sugar and brown sugar until smooth.
3. Beat in egg and vanilla.
4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.
5. Add all the dry ingredients.

Recipe: Grilled Cheese Sandwich
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
- 2 slices of bread
- 2 slices of cheese(cheddar or american)
- Butter

Instructions:
1. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
2. Butter one side of each bread slice
3. Place a slice of cheese btw buttered sides of bread.
4. Cook the sandwich in preheated skillet for 2-3 minutes until golden brown and cheese is melted.
5. Remove from heat and let it cool briefly before serving.
6. Enjoy your quick and tasty grilled cheese sandwich!

Recipe: Chicken Wraps
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
- 2 large flour tortillas
- 1 cup cooked, thinly sliced or shredded chicken
- 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
- 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
- 1/4 cup diced red onions
- 1/4 cup sliced cucumbers
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or sauce
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Lay tortillas flat.
2. Spread mayonnaise or sauce on each tortilla.
3. Layer chicken, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and cucumbers on one side.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Roll up tightly.
6. Cut in half, if desired.
7. Serve immediately or wrap in foil.
8. Enjoy these delicious chicken wraps!

Recipe: Mango Smoothie
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
- 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
- 1/2 cup plain yogurt or Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup orange juice
- 1/4 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
- Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:
1. Peel a ripe mango and cut it into small diced pieces.
2. Place the diced mango, yogurt, orange juice, milk, and sweetener (if desired) in a blender.
3. If you prefer a colder smoothie, add a few ice cubes.
4. Blend on high speed until all the ingredients are well combined and the smoothie is creamy and smooth.
5. Taste and adjust the sweetness if needed by adding more honey or maple syrup.
6. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
7. Enjoy this delicious and tropical mango smoothie!

Recipe: Lasagna
--------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:
- 9 lasagna noodles
- 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups ricotta cheese
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook noodles. Brown meat with onion and garlic. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper.
2. Mix ricotta, Parmesan, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl.
3. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
4. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella.
5. Bake covered at 375Â°F for 25 minutes, then uncovered for 10-15 minutes until golden.
6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy delicious lasagna!
